WHO WE ARE

Flower is the structure, that hosts provide reproductive organs in plants. A flower consists of 4 parts. In dicot plants, the flower cover consists of 2 parts: sepals and petals. A flower means a fragrant and beautifully colored part of the plant, a blooming garden plant, or a person whose behavior conforms to society.

One of the most important features of flowering plants is the presence of reproductive organs. At the same time, sexual reproduction occurs in flowering plants. Flowering plants have roots, stems and leaves. Flowering plants have ducts on the stem and leaves. With all their characteristics, flowering plants differ from non-flowering plants.

HOW WE APPROACH

CACTUS APPROACH

The cactus plant, which can live comfortably even in desert conditions, comes from the succulent plant family. Cactus; It grows in both North and South America, Northern Canada and even the African continent. There are very large cacti as well as small cacti. The cactus is thick-skinned to prevent water loss

Cactus is a drought tolerant plant and over watering will cause the cactus to rot. Since the minerals in its soil will be depleted, its soil should be changed every two years. It likes direct sunlight

You can water the cactus twice a day in spring and summer. Watering your indoor cactus once a month is sufficient. You should water the cactus from the base. You should not spray cacti with water, because they are desert plants, they are not accustomed to moisture
ROSE APPROACH

It is a beautiful looking plant from the rose family. The homeland of many roses is Asia. However, because it is such a showy plant, it is grown almost all over the world. There are about 25 varieties of roses in our country. The name of the shrub type plants that usually shed their leaves in winter and live for many years are called roses

In our country, Isparta is an important region in terms of rose cultivation. Most of the rose cultivation in Turkey is done here. It is an important foreign trade product in the field of agriculture. Syrup, vinegar and jam are made from the roses grown here. Rose oil obtained by distillation is used in perfumes, soaps and creams

According to its flowers; single layer roses, semi layered roses and layered roses. According to their height; They are dwarf, clinging-ivy and tall roses. According to the blooming times; Roses that bloom once a year are roses that bloom more than once a year. According to their colors; roses in white, red, pink, yellow, blue, black and salmon
